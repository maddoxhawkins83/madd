The real estate market provides plenty of opportunities for investors. Whether you are someone who is looking to invest in vacation homes, rental properties, house flipping or any other type of real estate, the returns can be quite lucrative when planned correctly. However, because real estate typically involves large initial investments, the process of investing should not be done haphazardly. Instead, it takes being familiar with the industry in order to get the best returns. If you are interested in investing in real estate and looking to learn more before you get started, here are the top 10 most influential real estate investment books that you should read.

#10 The Ultimate Beginner’s Guide To Real Estate Investing
What better place to begin your real estate investment books training, then with a comprehensive look into the basics of the industry? With The Ultimate Beginners Guide To Real Estate Investing, you will find a huge variety of information that will help you find the perfect place to begin your investment portfolio. This book will teach you how to get your first investment and how to turn that into future home purchases as well. The Ultimate Beginners Guide To Real Estate Investment is a vital book for building a foundation in the real estate market.

#9 Real Estate Investing For Dummies
You may not be dumb, but maybe you’re lacking a bit of knowledge in the real estate industry. That’s not a problem when you read Real Estate Investing For Dummies. This offers another comprehensive look into a variety of different real estate strategies from the ground up. And because it comes with the trusted “For Dummies” brand that offers a variety of different beginner books, it’s easily outlined and provides the most basic of information. Once you’ve finished reading this book, you’ll no longer be a dummy when it comes to real estate investment.

#8 The Four-Hour Workweek
This book isn’t geared towards real estate specifically, but it will teach you how to make the most of your time to maximize your effort. Many people get into real estate with the hopes of breaking the monotony of their regular 9-5 jobs. If you can get into real estate investment and start building a profitable portfolio, you can break free from your boring desk job. And who wouldn’t be happy when they can work four-hours a week and make better money than ever before? It takes a strong personality and will to break free from what others deem as normal. But with The Four-Hour Workweek, you’ll get on the right path.

#7 The Beginners Guide To Real Estate Investing
Some of the top reasons that people don’t invest in real estate is because they are afraid or concerned of what might happen with their investment, typically because of the negative energy others throw at them. However, with The Beginners Guide To Real Estate Investing, readers will learn how to avoid these types of mistakes by finding low-risk investment options. When it comes to real estate investment books, this one also outlines how to manage rental properties and make improvements to your homes, without burning a hole in your wallet.

#6 Rich Dad Poor Dad
One of the most popular books in the industry, Rich Dad Poor Dad explains the differences in how finances can be managed in your life, and the impact that can be made on your overall lifestyle. Robert Kiyosaki has a variety of different books out there –a few others make this list- that will help people turn modest gains into even bigger returns. You may have heard about this book because of the publicity it gets, but it also offers plenty of valuable insight that should not go overlooked.

#5 Real Estate Finance And Investment Manual
Once you start getting properties under your belt and start making money, it can be difficult to manage all of your finances and keep everything on the up-and-up. However, with the Real Estate Finance And Investment Manual, you’ll have strategies and resources that help you manage and diversify your money. The book has been updated since it first came out in 1977, so it continues to offer valuable information that involves newer markets and trending financial conditions.

#4 Landlording On Autopilot
Wouldn’t it be great if you owned a few different investment properties and could just sit back and watch the money flow in? With Landlording on Autopilot, you can work towards that goal. This book will teach you how to market your properties to get the best tenants, so that the work is minimal on your end. If concerns that being a landlord is too troublesome and takes too much effort, this book will help you overcome those worries and prepare for a variety of different situations you may come across.

#3 The Millionaire Real Estate Investor
Gary Keller is the owner of Keller Williams, one of the most prosperous real estate companies in the nation. In his book, The Millionaire Real Estate Investor, he outlines how he turned his single office operation into the corporate machine that it is today. Of all the real estate investment books, this one is easy to read and easy to understand, but it also provides plenty of deep information that will help individuals look for bigger returns on their investments.

#2 The Cash Flow Quadrant
Another Robert Kiyosaki book to make the list, The Cash Flow Quadrant offers further analysis into financial management when it comes to investment. This book looks further into the four important areas of investment, including Security, Wealth Building, Independence and Financial Freedom. This quadrant formula helps investors arrange their finances with their properties in order to make more from their initial investment.

#1 The ABCs Of Real Estate Investing
The books on this list started out with progressive information for beginners or even intermediate investors, but The ABC’s Of Real Estate Investing will help turn you into an expert investor. This book teaches you how to look for things that the average investor might miss, which can turn into serious rewards for you. The more of these hidden gems you find, the quicker your investments will turn into more lucrative opportunities.

Real estate can provide plenty of financial gains for those that know the industry. And in order to familiarize yourself, be sure to check out the books mentioned on this list.
